    The cornerstone of democracy is choice. Democratic government should be a dynamic toolset of, by and for the people. Often, ours are more metastasizing tools of entrenched interests.     At FiveBits, our raison d’etre is opportunity decentralization. In choosing each project, we consider ways that it might be used to, essentially, help others help themselves.

    Power consolidation is a global concern today. Power bases which inhibit the natural spread of opportunities are being cemented, wasting resourceful humans. Our governing systems seem designed to worsen this. As they metastatically grow, they tend to progressively obstruct more individual and community-mesh solutions.

    These designs are particularly at odds with the world today. Readily available technologies offer unparalleled options for individuals and communities to drive opportunities; to reduce reliance on top-down systems, while maintaining desired benefits.

    From neighborhoods to nations, our top-down hierarchical government paradigms often fail at their core functions. They cannot keep pace with population growth, evolutions of lifestyles and culture, or technological changes. They are brittle to change; neither scalable nor dynamic.

    These paradigms tend to imbue governments with little ability to consider bottom up drivers. The resultant systems invite waste and corruption, and often favor the voices of some select few over the voices of the denizens they are meant to serve. They are riddled with perverse incentives and divisive by design, if not goal; leaving politicians and populace alike with little reasonable recourse.

    Catastrophic foundational flaws, while esoteric in nature, are yet the levers of corruption and fundamental drivers of visible downstream issues. Addressing these means nothing less than reconceptualizing our governing system designs to better suit our peoples’ intended government goals.

    Democracy, from neighborhoods to nations, promises that the people have complete control over the fundamental framework of their government.

    We will bring that promise to reality.

    Fundamentally, our core constructive democracy toolkit supports the people defining their constitution, and being able to efficiently evolve it per strong majority.     Modern technology and design allows us to look beyond these core functions to providing peripheral government functions without command hierarchies, power centralization or corruption.
